ABOUT US

Shiequinn Tea is a modern tea house that offers a variety of matcha and hojicha drinks. We are dedicated to providing the best quality tea and ingredients to our customers. Our tea is sourced from Japan and Taiwan, and we offer a variety of flavors and toppings to choose from. We also offer a selection of vegan and dairy-free options. Our tea is made fresh to order and we use only the finest ingredients. We are committed to providing our customers with the best tea experience possible.
